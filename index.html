Why Cardio Fitness?

 Have you faced breathing issues when you lift heavy weight? sweats out when you do heavy works? All these are the indication of our body that it lacks oxygen. Cardio vascular system of our body is responsible for transportation of oxygen to every cell of our body, through blood. The word, aerobic stands for “with oxygen”. Aerobic exercises are focussed on nourishing your heart and cardio vascular system which consists of blood vessels.
 Aerobic exercises are the physical activity which makes you feel the same as you do heavy works. Your body experiences the same condition of lack of oxygen, so that we are training our body to handle such condition gently. Regular exercises makes improved blood circulation and helps to keep you healthy.
